--- title: Anxiety Reading date: '2009-08-24' tags: [] wp:post_id: '694' link: http://island94.dev/2009/08/anxiety-reading/ wp:post_type: post --- Fiction writer Dawes Green was on Studio360 on Saturday night and he made mention of how people have changed since he last published a novel 14 years ago:
People aren’t reading books so much. They text and Twitter and Google a lot—anxiety reading—but they’re too jumpy for books.
(I slightly lie: that quote above is from a Publishers Weekly interview, via Literary License blog, but it was close enough to what he said on the radio)
