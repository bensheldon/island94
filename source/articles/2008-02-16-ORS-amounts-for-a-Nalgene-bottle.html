--- title: ORS amounts for a Nalgene bottle date: '2008-02-16' tags: - disease - food - sick wp:post_id: '200' link: http://island94.dev/2008/02/ors-amounts-for-a-nalgene-bottle/ wp:post_type: post --- For a 32oz Nalgene Bottle (the standard biggish one) of clean water, the Oral Rehydration Salt quantities are:
  • Slightly less than 1/2 teaspoon of Salt (0.43tsp. to be exact)
  • 7.5 teaspoons of Sugar
(Personal note: when you've made it correctly, it should taste like minusculey-sweet saliva) Adapted from Rehydrate.org's ORS Recipe based on 1 liter of water. And the wikipedia entry on Oral Rehydration Salts (ORS)
