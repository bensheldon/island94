--- title: History is an art form rooted in scholarship date: '2010-05-09' tags: - history - ideology wp:post_id: '1913' link: http://island94.dev/2010/05/history-is-an-art/ wp:post_type: post --- A personal statement from a Public History grad student (taken from their Facebook Page): > I am interested in using history as an instrument for social change; history with a pragmatic purpose. I believe history is an art form rooted in scholarship.
